Our History

From Family Roots to Institutional Partner

Western Cape Stationers was acquired in 1999 by Moenadia Rooi Moosa — a decision rooted in entrepreneurship, service, and a deep commitment to community. What began as a local paper supplier has grown into a trusted B2B partner for government departments and corporate clients across the Western Cape.

Our core values of reliability, service excellence, and competitive pricing have never changed. Every order is managed personally by our family team, ensuring consistency and care that larger suppliers cannot match.

We hold a Level 1 B-BBEE certification and have an established track record with clients including the Western Cape Department of Social Development and the City of Cape Town.

We built this business on trust. When a client places an order with us, they know it will arrive on time, at the right price, and exactly as requested. That promise has carried us for 25 years and will carry us for 25 more.

👩‍💼
Moenadia Rooi Moosa
